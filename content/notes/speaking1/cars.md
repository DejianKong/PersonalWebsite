1. **Do you think car colors are important?**

Yes, of course. I do think car colours matter a lot. Black and white cars are the most common ones in daily life. But if people choose more unique colours like pink or red, their cars will look really eye-catching and stand out from others.

2. **Do you enjoy traveling by car when you were a kid?**

Yes, of course, I really enjoyed travelling by car when I was little. When I was a child, my parents often drove me to nearby cities for holidays. Travelling by car was really convenient and flexible for my family. We could go anywhere we liked and enjoy the beautiful natural scenery along the way.

3. **What types of cars do you like?**

Oh, that's a tricky question. Actually, I prefer electric cars to traditional petrol cars. One main reason is that electric vehicles run on electricity instead of fossil fuels, so they're much more eco-friendly and help protect the environment. What's more, they're also more affordable to run than conventional cars, so ordinary people can easily afford them.

4. **Do you prefer to be a driver or a passenger?**

To be honest, I'd rather be a passenger than a driver. I started taking driving lessons three years ago, but as a postgraduate student, I rarely get the chance to drive a private car. That's why I mostly prefer being a passenger. Besides, I really enjoy watching skilled drivers drive smoothly and steadily on the road.

5. **What do you usually do when there is a traffic jam?**

When I get stuck in a traffic jam, I usually take out my phone and scroll through it casually. I think most people do the same thing. There's always plenty of news and content on social media, so whenever I have spare time or I'm stuck in traffic, I just browse my phone to kill time.
