1. **Do you wear a watch?**

Actually, I seldom wear a watch these days. Back in junior high school, I used to wear one all the time, because I needed to keep track of the time. But now that I'm at university, I barely wear a watch at all. I can just check the time on my phone or laptop whenever I need to.

2. **Have you ever got a watch as a gift?**

Yes, of course. I got a watch as a gift when I was in junior high school. I still remember clearly that it was from my mum. It was a sports watch, really lightweight and practical.

3. **Why do some people wear expensive watches?**

I think it is mainly because expensive watches are symbols of wealth and social status. Wealthy people can afford luxury watches, and it's a way for them to show off their social position and wealth.

4. **Do you think it is important to wear a watch? Why?**

Actually, I don't think it's necessary for people to wear a watch these days. In the past, people relied on watches to check the time, but now almost everyone has a smartphone or other electronic devices. We can easily check the time with modern technology, so wearing a watch is not essential anymore.
