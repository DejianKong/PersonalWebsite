 ## PART 2
**Question**

Describe a new law you would like to introduce in your country.

You should say:

what law it is

what changes this law brings

whether this new law will be popular

how you come up with this new law

and explain how you feel about this new law

**Answer**

The new law I’d like to introduce is an Environmental Protection Tax, also known as the Green Tax.

This law is designed to impose higher taxes on institutions and individuals that cause serious pollution to our air and water resources.

It will bring many positive changes. For one thing, more people will choose eco-friendly lifestyles. They will prefer public transport over driving private cars to avoid extra tax fees. For another, factories will try to cut down harmful gas emissions. They will invest more money in upgrading equipment to reduce pollution.

I believe this new law will be very popular. These days, people’s environmental awareness is rising rapidly. Most citizens are willing to protect nature and support green policies.

I came up with this law because many other countries have already introduced similar green tax policies. I think it is practical and necessary for our country as well.

Personally, I think this is a meaningful law. It can effectively protect the environment, improve people’s physical and mental health, and also allow future generations to enjoy a clean and beautiful natural world.

 ## PART 3
1. **What rules should students follow at school?**

Actually, I think there are various rules that students should follow at school. First of all, they should go to school on time and finish their homework every day. This is because punctuality and completing school assignments can help them make steady progress in their studies and gain useful knowledge and practical skills. Besides, they ought to take part in after-school activities regularly, which is beneficial to their physical health and helps them keep fit.

2. **Do people in your country usually obey the law?**

Yes, of course. Generally speaking, Chinese people are willing to obey the law. One main reason is that anyone who breaks the law or commits a crime has to take responsibility and face serious punishment, such as fines or even imprisonment. Therefore, most people try their best to follow social rules and laws. They want to keep themselves and their family away from legal trouble and avoid any negative consequences in life.

3. **What kinds of behavior are considered as good behavior?**

Actually, I think there are many kinds of good behaviour in daily life. First, people should follow laws and traffic rules. For example, we should only cross the road or drive when the traffic light is green. In this way, we can avoid traffic jams and prevent hurting other people. Besides, taking part in voluntary work is also regarded as good behaviour. It allows us to help people who are in need and bring warmth to society.

4. **Do you think children can learn about law outside of school?**

Yes, of course. I do believe that children can learn about the law outside of school. Nowadays, there are many TV programs that introduce basic legal knowledge. When kids watch these shows, they can learn about different laws and the consequences of breaking them. Besides, children can also learn a lot from their parents. For example, when parents drive or cross the road only when the traffic light turns green, kids can gradually build their awareness of traffic safety and other social rules.

5. **What are the benefits of people obeying rules?**

Actually, I think one of the main benefits for people to obey rules is that it can help them protect themselves from potential risks. Take traffic rules as an example. If people follow traffic rules and cross the road only when the light turns green, they can avoid traffic accidents and protect their personal safety. Besides, obeying rules is good for social harmony. For instance, if factories follow environmental regulations and control harmful gas emissions, we can better protect the environment and let everyone live a healthier life.

6. **How can parents teach children to obey rules?**

I think there are various ways parents can teach children to obey rules. Firstly, they can buy books about basic rules and read them to kids before bedtime. In this way, children can learn about common rules and the results of breaking them. Besides, parents should set a good role model for their kids. For example, when parents drive or cross the road only when the traffic light is green, children can gradually raise their awareness of traffic safety and learn to follow rules in daily life.
